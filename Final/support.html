Donate

Donate to help fund the mission of The Pitbull Haven. We accept monetary donations, supplies such as blankets, beds, sweaters, bowls, collars, shampoo, and leashes, food both wet and dry, and cleaning supplies.

Volunteer

Help support The Pitbull Haven by becoming a Volunteer. We need staff volunteers that take animals for walks, bathe, feed, play, and cleaning. Volunteers are also needed to be foster homes for the dogs until adoption.

Educate

TPH encourages everyone to educate and share our mission to get the word out to others. Bring awareness about the misinformation surrounding pitbulls and put an end to Breed-specific legislation and breed-discriminatory legislation.
